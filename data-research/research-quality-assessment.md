❌ Nonprofit outcomes scattered

## What This Means for MVP
### Build with (High Confidence):
- County SVI + preventable hosp bivariate map
- Health system maturity matrix (Trinity north star)
- FQHC locations + quality scores
- State hub + CIE network map
- 211 demand + housing context
### Flag as Phase 2:
- County readmit rates (2018-2019)
- County-level 211 breakdowns
- Health system outcomes (Trinity yes, others pending)
### Don't Build (No Data):
- County CHW headcount comparison
- County hub funding visualization
- Transportation accessibility index
- Nonprofit SDOH intervention outcomes

"211 shows statewide demand. County breakdown coming soon."
## Research Process Lessons
### What Worked
- Sequential team approach (narrative → data → gaps)
- Explicit quality flags (year, completeness %)
- Phase 2 roadmap with realistic timelines
- Specific numbers > estimates
### Next Cycle
- Data availability audit first
- Phase 2 outreach in parallel
- Validate published numbers with sources
- Separate "best available" from "gold standard"
