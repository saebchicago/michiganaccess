# Gemini Deep Research Findings
**Date:** March 2026
**Focus:** Strategic narrative, policy context, health system equity maturity
---
## Core Finding
Michigan's SDOH infrastructure is transitioning from episodic interventions to holistic framework. 80% of health outcomes determined by non-clinical factors. State has established robust SDOH hubs with community-led governance, CIE Task Force working on interoperability, and health systems with varying levels of maturity in SDOH screening/intervention.
---
## MDHHS 2024-2027 SDOH Strategy
"Roadmap to Healthy Communities" - structural intervention approach
**10 prosperity regions** as organizing framework
**SDOH Hubs Advisory Council** issued 42 recommendations (Jan 2024):
- Permanent governance structure
- Integrated care coordination
- Sustainable funding models
---
## CIE (Community Information Exchange)
Deliberately vendor-agnostic approach (unlike single-platform states)
Multiple active platforms:
- Connect4Care (UWSEM, SE Michigan)
- Healthify/WellSky (BCBSM statewide)
- ThumbCares.org (Thumb region)
- County-level CHIRs (Genesee, Northern MI, Jackson, Livingston-Washtenaw)
---
## Health System Initiatives
### Corewell Health
- "Healthier Communities" initiative
- Regionalized equity focus
- MAPP framework for community needs assessment
### Henry Ford Health
- 1M+ SDOH screens
- Documented gap in need detection (4.3% via MAs)
- CHW-centered approach with dedicated hub
- 7 school-based + 10 community-based sites in Detroit
### Trinity Health Michigan
- IOTA model (ESRD + SDOH equity)
- Multi-county CHW programs
- Documented outcomes (16% hosp reduction)
### Michigan Medicine
- Academic center
- Strong CHNA process
- 17.1% screening positive rate (above typical)
### McLaren, Sparrow, Bronson, Munson, UP Health
- Varying levels of SDOH documentation in CHNAs
---
## Key Insight
**The Detection Gap:** Michigan screens millions for social needs but lacks unified post-screening data infrastructure. Systems and nonprofits exist, but they operate in silos. The platform opportunity: connect the dots between screening, intervention, and outcomes.
---
## Strategic Recommendation
Position Access Michigan as the **unifying layer** that helps:
- Health systems benchmark against Trinity's outcomes
- MDHHS track hub performance across regions
- Nonprofits coordinate with health systems
- Communities understand where to get help
