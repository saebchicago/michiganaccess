## Federal Policy Context
**Michigan:** NO HRSN 1115 waiver (unlike CA, NY, OR, AZ, AR, MA, NJ)
**Alternative:** State Plan authority for CHW Medicaid reimbursement (Jan 1, 2024)
**Impact:** Less SDOH funding than waiver states. Trump admin rescinded Biden HRSN guidance (Mar 2025).
